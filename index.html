welcome to sharma bekrey


Welcome to sharma bekerey, where every treat is made with love and the finest ingredients. We specialize in crafting fresh, handcrafted baked goods, from artisan breads and buttery croissants to decadent cakes and pastries. Our passion for baking shines through in every bite, and we’re committed to delivering a warm, welcoming experience for every customer. Whether you're here for a daily delight or a special occasion, we’re excited to share our creations with you!

At sharma bekerey, our signature product is our *freshly baked sourdough bread*, which has become a local favorite. Made with a blend of high-quality flour, natural fermentation, and a touch of care, it offers a perfect balance of flavor and texture – with a crisp, golden crust and a soft, airy interior. It’s the result of hours of slow fermentation and craftsmanship, and it's perfect for everything from a simple sandwich to pairing with soups and salads. Whether you’re a long-time sourdough fan or new to the experience, this bread is a must-try and a testament to our bakery’s dedication to quality.
